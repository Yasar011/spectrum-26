Frequently Asked Questions

Is Accommodation provided?

Yes, basic hostel accommodation is available for ₹300/night. Contact the Accommodation Head to book.

Can I register on the spot?

Yes, but online registration is recommended to avoid queues and secure merchandise.

Are pro-shows free?

Pro-shows are free for Nexus Prime holders. Basic pass holders need an add-on ticket.

What is the Refund Policy?

All ticket sales are final. No refunds will be processed under any circumstances.
